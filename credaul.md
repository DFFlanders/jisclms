# Project Overview #
  * **Full Name of Project**: Combining Resource Discovery Across University Libraries
    * **Project Tag**: CReDAUL
  * **Project Descriptions** (how would you describe this project to your neighbour?):
    * **short**:  Using an Open Source Resource Discovery Application to contain the records of Sussex and Brighton Universities
    * **long**: The project will provide a seamless, overarching interface for students and staff of the Brighton and Sussex Medical School (BSMS) to simultaneously search the library holdings of the University of Sussex (UoS) and the University of Brighton (UoB). We will select and implement an Open Source next generation resource discovery tool to contain all the records of the two university libraries. A senior academic in Human-Computer Interaction (and his students) from the School of Informatics at the University of Sussex will lead on the user requirement and usability testing of ensure the proposed solution is fit for purpose.
